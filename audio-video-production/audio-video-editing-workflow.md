# Audio and Video Editing Workflow

Tools used:
Audacity
Shotcut
OpenShot
Ardour

Workflow:
1. Import raw audio or video files
2. Remove background noise
3. Normalize audio levels
4. Cut and arrange video clips
5. Export final media format

Applications:
These workflows are used in multimedia production,
podcast editing, video content creation,
and AI training datasets involving audio and visual media.

This process demonstrates structured digital media editing
and production techniques.
